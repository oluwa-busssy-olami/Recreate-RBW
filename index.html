About Us

At Renbi Women Empowerment Initiative, we prioritize the advancement of women and firmly believe that
empowering women is an absolute necessity in creating a truly equitable and just society.

Our Mission

To equip Nigerian women with the resources necessary to attain financial independence and reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To create sustainable economic opportunities for women in disadvantaged communities in Nigeria

Our Aim and Objectives

To support women in taking control of their finances and making smart financial decisions that benefit themselves and their families.

What We Do

At Renbi Women Empowerment Initiative, we are dedicated to empowering women in vulnerable communities across Nigeria. Our goal is to equip them with the resources and support necessary to achieve financial independence and unlock their full potential through our initiatives and programs.

What Renbi?

Investing in the empowerment of women has the potential to create a positive ripple effect that extends beyond their own lives and benefits their families, communities and society at large.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a Registered NGO

Renbi Women Empowerment Initiative is registered as an incorporated trustee under Part F of the Companies and Allied Matters Acts (CAMA) 2020.

What is the primary focus of Renbi women?

Our primary focus is to empower women in disadvantaged communities in Nigeria by providing them with access to education and economic opportunities to be financially independent.

What kind of program does Renbi Women offer to empower women?

We offer a range of programs that mainly focus on entrepreneurship and education. You can learn more about our programs here.

How do Renbi women help women get financially independent?

We help women achieve financial independence by providing them with materials and equipment. In addition, we cover the cost of their vocational training in their chosen field. Our goal is to give them a financial head start to start or expand their business.
